About us

Saint Peter's Preparatory School's FIRST® Robotics Competition Team, The Technological Terrors, is a 501(c)(3) organization located in Jersey City, New Jersey. Our robotics team is a way for students to have an outlet for their passions in competitions against other teams through the medium of STEM, inspiring a new generation of thinkers in engineering. We also provide opportunities for students from diverse backgrounds to express their passions and interests — in fact more than half the team is an underrepresented minority — and to join and collaboratively learn and have fun.

Beyond robotics, our mission emphasizes a diverse learning culture, teamwork, leadership, and gracious professionalism. We're also dedicated to upholding the unique Jesuit values of being men for others, striving for excellence in all things, and care for the whole person.

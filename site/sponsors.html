Team 5438


Each year, from January to April, we participate in the FRC competition series, where we build a robot from scratch to compete against other high schools worldwide. During the six-week building time, our students must design, build, wire, and program our unique robot to complete the assigned task, while also fundraising and managing our finances. With materials, registration, and travel costs, prices add up quickly, and our school does not fully cover our expenses.

We are committed to being accessible to all students of all experience levels and of all socioeconomic backgrounds. With your help, we can hopefully continue to fulfill our mission and provide students with the robotics experience of a lifetime.

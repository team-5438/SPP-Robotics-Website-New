Despite being affiliated with Saint Peter's Preparatory School, SPP Robotics is NOT a religious or for-profit organization. The funds obtained will be used exclusively for SPP Robotics. With these resources, our leadership team will guide and assist other members in designing and building the best robot we can. We hope you will consider giving the team your grant and helping us build these skills, our robot, and our community.
